BIO

My name is Glenn Rockwood, an alumnus of Olympic College, where I studied Director of Photography and Director. I am now pursuing filmmaking full-time, following several years dedicated to digital art in 3D media — animation, modeling, and virtual VFX.

I have been credited on several film sets as Director and as 1st Assistant Camera, and I continue to seek out new collaborations and productions that expand my experience behind the camera and on set. Based on Bainbridge Island, Washington, I am committed to learning more, collaborating more, and growing one project at a time.
